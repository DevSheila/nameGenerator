About

Ever wondered what your name would be if you were Ghanian? Well, you can find that out whith this Akan Name Generator.

Akans of Ghana have a fairly unique naming system, where the first name of a child is based on the day he/she is born, and on the gender of the child. So a boy born on a Monday would be named Kwadwo or Koho, and a girl would be called Adwoa. Each day has one or more variants, but there's usually not much to choose from.
